FAQ
What do you actually charge?
Sliding scale. Small shows might be for gas money, we're looking to grow our portfolio and love what we do. Big festivals are a different conversation. We'll be honest about what's realistic for your budget.
What kind of shows?
If you're making something real, we want to help it land. We amplify, accentuate, and accelerate.
What if my budget is really small?
Talk to us anyway. We're not going to price out the scene we care about. We'll figure something out or we'll be honest that we can't make it work.
How far do you travel?
We have transport. We'll factor travel into the sliding scale. If you're in the DMV, we're probably game.
Can you do just one thing — like only sound?
Yes. You don't need the full stack. If you just need a PA and a competent pair of hands, lfg.
